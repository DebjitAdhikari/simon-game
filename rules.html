Rules:

The computer will click one color at a time at each stage. To play the game, you must be able to recall every color in the right order. Here's an illustration:

Level:1

Computer: Red
Player: Red ☑️

Level:2

Computer: Yellow
Player: Red, Yellow ☑️

Level:3

Computer: Blue
Player: Red, Yellow, Blue ☑️

Level:4

Computer: Red
Player: Red, Yellow, Red ❌
